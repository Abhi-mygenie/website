# SECTION 1 — MASTER PRODUCTION GUIDE
### Applies to all 17 pieces

---

## Brand Voice
- **Tone:** Direct, confident, no fluff. Speak like a sharp friend who knows restaurants.
- **Never say:** "Powerful", "Seamless", "Revolutionary", "Game-changer", "Next-gen"
- **Always say:** Specific numbers, real outcomes, real customer names where available
- **Language:** English captions/VO. Indian context throughout (₹, Indian names, Indian city names)

## The 3-Second Rule
- **First 3 seconds = the PAIN.** Show the problem, not the product.
- If the viewer doesn't feel "that's me" in 3 seconds, they scroll away.
- Every video starts with a relatable frustration — not a logo.

## Audience Profile
Restaurant/café/hotel owner in India. 30–55 years old. Running 1–5 outlets.
- Busy. Distrustful of software that promises too much.
- Responds to: real numbers, real names, real problems they face daily.
- Skeptical of: generic demos, foreign contexts, inflated claims.
- Decision trigger: "If this catches ₹1 lakh in theft — I need this."
